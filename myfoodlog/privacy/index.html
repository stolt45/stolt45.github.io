My Food Log

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

The short version

My Food Log is designed to keep your data on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking.

Data storage

All food log entries, dietary categories, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to us or any third party by default.

Optional AI categorization

My Food Log offers an optional feature that uses the Anthropic API to categorize foods. This feature is entirely opt-in and requires you to provide your own Anthropic API key. When enabled, the name of a food item is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's servers for categorization. No other data is sent. We do not operate any intermediary servers and have no access to your API key or the data you send to Anthropic. Anthropic's use of that data is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy.

No tracking

My Food Log does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies. We do not collect usage data of any kind.

No account required

The app does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no cloud sync.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at privacy@stolt45.com.
